1. About the Forum
Organization Name: UIU English Language Forum (UIUELF)
Affiliation: A Wing of the Directorate of Career Counseling & Student Affairs (DCCSA)
Established: September 20, 2013
Motto: "Break fear and be confident."
Mission & Vision
The UIU English Language Forum is a premier student body at United International University dedicated to bridging the gap between academic learning and professional communication. We believe that English proficiency is not just about language—it is a tool for leadership, career advancement, and global connectivity.
Our mission is to cultivate a thriving ecosystem where students can overcome the fear of public speaking, master corporate communication, and develop critical soft skills. Through a diverse array of seminars, interactive workshops, competitions, and study circles, we empower students to become articulate, confident, and career-ready professionals.
Signature Program: English Edge
English Edge is the flagship skill development initiative of UIUELF. It is a comprehensive training module designed to take students from basic proficiency to advanced professional communication.
Core Focus: Public Speaking, Corporate Grooming, Vocabulary Building, and Creative Writing.
Format: Intensive workshops led by industry experts and renowned trainers.
Objective: To ensure every participant leaves with the tangible skills needed to excel in job interviews, presentations, and cross-cultural environments.
Leadership
Moderator: Dr. Muhammad Fazle Ramzan Khan
Role: Associate Professor, Department of English, UIU.
Tenure: Assumed leadership in early 2024, steering the club toward a new era of linguistic excellence and dynamic engagement.
